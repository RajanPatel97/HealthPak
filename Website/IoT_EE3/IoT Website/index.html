The smart cold box

When shipping sensitive goods such as medicines, organs or even food, we need to make sure that they are transported in the optimal conditions. ColdBox is a tool for suppliers and consumers to accurately monitor the transport environment of these goods.

Find Out More

We've got what you need!

By continoulsy measuring ambient conditions using a collection of sensors, our complete cold box monitoring device shares data online, allowing the user to easily access this information at any given time and warning them if anything goes wrong.

At Your Service

Temperature

Ambient and surface temperature sensors combined with a humidity sensor ensures perfect transport conditions.

Vibrations

An accelerometer tracks the vibrations of the goods, allowing the device to send out a warning when certain limits are exceeded.

Up to Date

Frequent measurements provide accurate and updated results.

Low power

Our robust design has very low power consumption. No need to worry about batteries.
